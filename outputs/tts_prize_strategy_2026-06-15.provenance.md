# Provenance — TTS Prize Strategy (2026-06-15)

## Primary sources consulted

1. Build Small Hackathon Field Guide
   - URL: https://build-small-hackathon-field-guide.hf.space/
   - Key points used:
     - entry rule: each model must be under 32B,
     - sponsor prizes and OpenBMB MiniCPM eligibility,
     - sponsor-models must be a core part of the experience,
     - bonus badges such as `Off Brand`, `Best Demo`, `Best Agent`.

2. OpenBMB MiniCPM-o 2.6 model card
   - URL: https://huggingface.co/openbmb/MiniCPM-o-2_6
   - Key points used:
     - total size 8B,
     - configurable voices,
     - speech conversation,
     - voice cloning support,
     - published speech/voice-cloning claims and benchmark table.

3. hexgrad/Kokoro-82M model card
   - URL: https://huggingface.co/hexgrad/Kokoro-82M
   - Key points used:
     - 82M parameters,
     - Apache-2.0 license,
     - positioning as fast and cost-efficient open-weight TTS.

4. Kyutai Pocket-TTS model card
   - URL: https://huggingface.co/kyutai/pocket-tts
   - Key points used:
     - 100M parameters,
     - CPU-friendly,
     - voice cloning support,
     - client-side/browser claim,
     - prohibited-use restrictions.

5. Parler-TTS Mini v1 model card
   - URL: https://huggingface.co/parler-tts/parler-tts-mini-v1
   - Key points used:
     - prompt-controlled natural speech,
     - explicit control over gender, pitch, speed, and recording style,
     - specific speaker consistency support.
